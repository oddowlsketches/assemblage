Oracle Stack - Export Options

Oracle Stack allows you to export your oracle readings in various formats. Here are some examples of what the exports might look like:

Stack Layout Export

When exporting from the stack layout, your images will be arranged in a grid for clarity in the exported version. This ensures all images are fully visible in the final output.

Grid Layout Export

Grid layout exports maintain the same arrangement as seen on screen, with the fortune text displayed below the image grid.

Row Layout Export

Row layout exports arrange your images in a horizontal line, perfect for creating a narrative-style reading from left to right.

PDF Export

PDF exports include the date of your reading and are formatted for easy printing. They make great additions to journals or for sharing with friends.
